How It Works

Solv integrates the most popular and top freelancers in the region, from accountants to consultants, designers, developers, translators, writers, and more. These freelancers are screened and verified before their start at Solv. The majority are highly skilled and have a vast of experience that can help you solve what you need at any moment in time!

All you have to do is

01. Start by posting a job

• The job is what you require help in, for example, can be logo design, consulting on a contract, translation, and much more.
• Provide a full brief on what is required
• Include budget
• Include timeline

02. You have two options after a job has been posted:

A. Wait until you receive bids
B. Search for suitable freelancers and ask them to check out your project post! You can also favorite them.

03. If you have received bids - view proposals, interview, and analyze whichever you feel is suitable and matches your project post.

04. If you have sent out an invitation and received an acceptance - interview and analyze the freelancer and see whether he/she best fits.

05. Prepare the contract and list milestones based on what has been agreed on with the freelancer.

06. Release the payment when you have been fully satisfied with the project and work received!

Tips on choosing the right freelancer

• Check out the freelancer’s previous work
• Check out the ratings and freelancer’s feedback
• Contact the freelancer and screen him/her yourself and ask as many questions to ensure your project goals are aligned with his/her expertise

FAQ’s

Got Questions?! Here are some of the most common questions received and have been answered!

Are the freelancers screened?
Yes they are! Every freelancer’s profile has to be approved by the Solv admin team. We have several procedures in place to ensure that the quality of the freelancer is to the standard needed to complete jobs and hope to deliver what you need!

However, it is your responsibility to screen the freelancer beforehand, we suggest having a few questions, take a quick call or even set a meeting to discuss the project in full detail to ensure that they are best fit for the job.
How do I create a new project?
• Click on the “Post a new project” button on the top right of the page or click here.
• Select the category of choice and fill in all required information. 
• Tip: Be as clear as you can, if you are unsure of certain specifics, you can mention in the box that you require a call to discuss the project in order to list down the deliverables.
Can I pay with my credit card?
Yes, at the moment all payments will be done online either through Paypal or with your debit/credit card. Payments will be done directly prior to the start of the project and will be held in escrow. This will ensure that protection for yourself and the freelancer. Payment will only be released if you are fully satisfied.
What if I am not happy with the outcome of the
freelancer’s work? Can I request a refund?
If you are not happy, you are advised to ask the freelancer to do the revisions required. As well, you can resolve any disputes by contacting our Support Team.
I posted a project but I don't want to go ahead anymore.
How do I cancel or delete a project posted?
•  Go to "My Project"
•  Click on the project you want to cancel/delete
•  On the right-hand side of the page you can either close or delete the project. 
Can I sign up as a freelancer as well?
At the moment it is not possible, we are looking to integrate this feature soon. All you have to do is create a second account with a secondary e-mail.
